Brandcrush is a marketing platform that connects brands with thousands of activation spaces.
Why list an activation space?
Monetise existing foot traffic and help your customers discover brands they’ll love.
How does it work?

You can list a space and activate for a brand or list a space and have the brand activate themselves.

The types of Spaces you can list are endless, Brandcrush finds the brands so you have more time to get creative. Here’s some fun ideas to get you started:

  • List an Activation Space "On the Menu" at your cafe.
  • List an Activation Space "on counter tops" at gyms and yoga studios.
  • List an Activation Space as "Gift with Purchase" with fashion retailers.
  • List an Activation Space "in the mini bar" at hotels in every city or country town.
  • List an Activation Space in "goody bags" at school fetes.

How much should I charge?

You can charge per day, per hour or per activation depending on the type of space you are listing.

Charge as much or as little as you like, however most Activation Hosts are charging $0.50 to $1.50 per product reach. Spaces that distribute samples as a gift with purchase or provide a dedicated staff member can charge a premium. Note: Brandcrush take a 10% commission on all bookings.
